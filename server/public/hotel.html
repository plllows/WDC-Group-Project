    The information about the hotel:

    Located just 300 m from the Crown Casino, Experience Bella Hotel Apartments is ideally located to indulge in the restaurants, bars and shopping at Southbank. Guests enjoy free WiFi and free local and national phone calls.
    Experience Bella Hotel Apartments is located within a 15-minute walk of the Melbourne Convention Centre and the DFO Shopping Centre. Eureka Tower is 600 m from Bella Apartments. The nearest airport is Tullamarine Airport, 20 km from the property.
    The property offers a range of one and two bedroom apartments, many with unparalleled city and bay views. Each room is appointed with a microwave, fridge, dishwasher, washing machine, dryer, air conditioning, satellite TVs in the living and master bedroom and Internet access.
    Guests can enjoy a work out in the fitness centre.
